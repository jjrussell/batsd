Offerpal Media Powers Virtual Currency Monetization for MMA Pro Fighter


December 22, 2009

Digital Chocolate employs Offerpal's alternative payment platform for MMA Pro Fighter on Facebook

San Mateo, CA, - December 22, 2009 - Offerpal Media, the leading virtual currency monetization solution for developers and social publishers, today announced a partnership with leading game developer Digital Chocolate to incorporate offers and alternate payment options into MMA Pro Fighter™, a Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) fighting game that is now available on Facebook.

"MMA Pro Fighter is our first true social game, and with Offerpal's expertise we hope to hit the ground running," said Trip Hawkins, Founder and CEO of Digital Chocolate. "The platform will enable us to earn additional revenues while providing consumers more options to advance in this adrenaline-packed MMA experience."

Digital Chocolate will use Offerpal to enable MMA Pro Fighter players to earn the game's virtual currency, Pro Points, by taking part in demographically and behaviorally targeted consumer advertising offers, including free trials, big-brand discounts, shopping rewards, online surveys and more. Offerpal will also offer MMA Pro Fighter players the ability to purchase Pro Points by choosing from a variety of global payment options including mobile billing, pre-paid debit cards, e-wallets and more. Pro Points can be redeemed for in-game items, stamina and other virtual items to further enhance the experience and reward users for their engagement.

"We are extremely proud to add Digital Chocolate to our growing list of more than 2,000 publishing partners," said George Garrick, CEO of Offerpal Media.  "The decision by a company like Digital Chocolate to implement our solution is a testament to the fact that consumers are clearly excited about offer-based payment alternatives."

MMA Pro Fighter will give users the chance to issue a "beat down" as they compete against their friends to become the best fighter in the ring. Created specifically to take full advantage of Facebook, players will be able to build and develop their fighter and compete with friends to reach the top of the ranks.  Players can build their fighters and learn hundreds of moves from different fighting styles. MMA Pro Fighter features an in-depth fighting engine that realistically simulates fights based on authentic MMA moves and attributes.

MMA Pro Fighter is available to play for free on Facebook. Digital Chocolate will be implementing enhancements and upgrades for MMA Pro Fighter in the coming weeks so check back often to see the updates: http://apps.facebook.com/mmaprofighter/.

About Offerpal Media

Offerpal Media is the leader in virtual currency monetization for online games, virtual worlds and social networks. The company's turnkey payment platform gives consumers the opportunity to earn or buy virtual currency by completing targeted advertising offers or by purchasing it through direct, global payment options. Since its launch in 2007, Offerpal has issued more than 750 billion virtual points to more than 160 million consumers across 2,000 publishers. The company is headquartered in Fremont, California.
