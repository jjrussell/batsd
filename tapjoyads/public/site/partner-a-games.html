Offerpal Media Partners with Aeria Games to Monetize Virtual Currency, Increase User Engagement


March 11, 2009

Leading Social Gaming Publisher Taps Offerpal to Allow Users to Earn Aeria Points for Free

Fremont, CA - March 11, 2009 - Offerpal Media, the leading monetization platform for social applications, social networks, social games, virtual worlds and mobile applications, today announced a partnership with Aeria Games ,one of the Web's fastest growing free-to-play online gaming communities.

Offerpal Media already helps over 800 social web publishers earn eCPM rates in the hundreds of dollars. Now, Aeria Games, home to a 5 million+ member community and publisher of popular games including Last Chaos and Shaiya, similarly monetizes and engages its audience through Offerpal Media's Managed Offer Platform. By integrating its existing virtual currency system-Aeria Points-with Offerpal Media's platform, Aeria Games enables its users to earn Aeria Points for free in exchange for taking part in scientifically targeted advertising offers such as free trials, big-brand discounts, online surveys and more.

"This partnership is a major step forward for us in both engaging and monetizing our loyal user base," said Aeria Games CEO Lan Hoang. "It was extremely easy for us to deploy Offerpal's platform and begin generating incremental revenue from the in-game offers. Offerpal has not only improved user satisfaction by providing our audience with a new way to earn Aeria Points, but their solution has given us a significant new revenue stream as well."

As an alternate payment option, Offerpal Media provides millions of Aeria Games' users with a way to obtain Aeria Points for free, thereby allowing them to unlock more features and carry out more tasks within the many games offered by the company. With more Aeria Points in their account, users return more often and spend more time within the game.

The free-to-play online gaming industry is becoming an increasingly valuable space for both advertisers and publishers alike. With the help of Offerpal Media, Aeria Games is poised to become one of the most popular and diversified publishers in the space.

"Offerpal Media is committed to providing highly effective monetization solutions for the social Web, including gaming communities like Aeria Games," said Offerpal Media Founder and CEO Anu Shukla. "Aeria Games offers an exceptionally diverse and high quality range of games for their very large community of members. We are excited to partner with them and help generate real revenues from our offer network, while enhancing the gaming experience and engagement of Aeria's audience"
