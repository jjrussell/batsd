Customer Service Inquiries:

To better assist you, we ask that you provide the following: the application that you are using, social network, your profile URL, and your offer id (you can find this out from the view offer status page).

  • 1. Go to the main offers page of the application on which you experienced an issue.
  • 2. Click on the link for missing currency (found in the upper right, above the offers).
  • 3. Fill out the form on the ensuing page, and an Offerpal Media representative will respond to your request shortly.
  • 4. You may also contact support@offerpal.com.
